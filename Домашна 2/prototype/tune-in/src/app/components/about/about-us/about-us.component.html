About us

"I haven’t been everywhere, but it’s on my list”

TuneIn is a project of a team of random individuals with the same passion for exploration and adventure. Travelling the world isn’t easy during a pandemic, but with TuneIn, you can travel the world through the prism of music and discover locations while staying safe in the comfort of your own home.

And for the days when you feel a like you’re on the top of the world, or the ones when you’re a little under the weather, we care. So tune in to receive carefully chosen jams to accompany you throughout the whole day.
